How We Teach

Teaching Methods That Actually Work

Our pedagogy is built on 13 years of teaching experience, refined into a system that takes you from hesitant speaker to confident communicator.

Signature Method

⚡ Marathon Grammar

The crown jewel of English Mind's curriculum. Marathon Grammar is not your standard grammar class — it is a high-intensity, pattern-based training system designed to bypass the "translate in your head" habit that plagues most English learners.

Through rapid-fire drills, timed responses, and real-world sentence construction, students develop grammatical reflexes. You stop thinking about grammar — you just speak correct English naturally.

Four dedicated marathon grammar batches run daily: 6:00–6:40 PM, 6:50–7:30 PM, 7:40–8:20 PM, and 9:00–9:40 PM.

📌 What You Will Learn

  • All 12 English tenses — used naturally in speech
  • Sentence structures for different situations
  • Question formation and answer delivery
  • Conditional sentences and advanced grammar
  • Error correction reflexes

✅ Expected Outcome

Within 90 days, students report speaking in grammatically correct sentences without consciously thinking about rules — the ultimate goal of Marathon Grammar.
